English grammar - 04 定语和定语从句

FishGeeker Jun 7 2021-06-07T22:10:00+02:00
Jun 8 2021-06-08T17:11:23+02:00 7 min

什么是定语

只要听到。。。的加名词，。。。的就是修饰这个名词的定语成分。

定语的成分

  • 形容词修饰名词
    • The innocent nightingale died.
    • The nightingale brave and innocent died.
  • 名词修饰名词
    • The nightingale’s singing can make the rose bloom. ✅❎
    • The singing of the nightingale can make the rose bloom. ✅
  • 介词短语做定语
    • The nightingale out of the window heard the sigh of the youngster.
  • 非谓语动词做定语
    • The singing nightingale lost his life.
    • The nightingale to sing lost his life.
  • 从句修饰名词
  • 形容词性物主代词

定语的位置

前小后大：

  • 当一个单词修饰名词的时候通常放在名词前，当多个单词修饰名词的时候通常放在名词后。
  • 当过去分词修饰名词的时候，即使只有一个单词，也通常放在名词后

练习

  • 非谓语动词修饰名词
    • 那个懂爱的年轻人被拒绝了。The youngster grasping romance was declined.
  • 介词短语修饰名词
    • 我有一朵和白云一样白的玫瑰花。A rose as white as clouds remains available for me.
  • 被扔掉的玫瑰花是红色的。The rose discarded looks red.
  • 这是一个关于夜莺和玫瑰的故事。This proves a fiction about a nightingale and a rose.

定语从句

构成

定语从句：名词 + 引导词 + 句子

引导词

定语从句的引导词是按照先行词的种类分的类，一共把引导词分成五类：

  • 当先行词是人的时候，引导词有：who, whom, whose
  • 当先行词是物的时候，引导词有：that, which, whose
  • 当先行词是时间的时候，引导词有：that, which, when
  • 当先行词是地点：that, which, where
  • 当先行词是原因：that, which, why

谁决定引导词的用法

  • 先行词

  • 引导词在从句中能够充当的成分

  • I will never forget the day when I met you.
  • I will never forget the day that we spent.
  • You had better have no reason why you are late.
  • You had better have no reason that sounds perfect.

引导词的分类新标准

定语从句的引导词还可以按照引导词本身的词性来分类，一共把引导词分成三类：

  • 代词（在从句中一定要充当主语或者宾语的成分）：who, whom, that, which
  • 副词（在从句不能充当任何主干成分）：where, when, why
  • 形容词（在从句中修饰离它最近的名词）：whose

练习

  • 夜莺用生命换来的玫瑰花被扔掉了。The rose that/which the nightingale exchanged with his life was dumped.
  • 这个老人在他儿子到达的那天去世了。The senior citizen passt away on the day when her son arrived.
  • 我喜欢王子送给我的珠宝。The gift that the prince sent to me never fails to fascinate me.

定语从句的特殊用法

  • 先行词和引导词之间有介词的话，人不能用who，只能用whom，物不用that，只能用which。
    • I will never forget the day on which I met you.
    • Mr. Wang is a man from whom we should learn.
  • that引导的定语从句，如果在从句中充当宾语，that可以省略
    • The gift the prince sent to me never fails to fascinate me.
  • 区别the same…as; the same…that
    • He is the same man as I love. 他像我爱的那个人。
    • He is the same man that I love. 他就是我爱的那个人。
  • 区别限制和非限制性定语从句
    • 有逗号的是非限制定语从句
    • 非限制性定语从句相当于插入语，可以完全不看
      • I love watermelons, which are round. 我喜欢西瓜。（西瓜可圆可方）
      • I love watermelons which are round. 我喜欢圆的西瓜。
  • which引导的非限制定语从句可以修饰前面整一句话，as也可以，但是as引导的非限制性定语从句既可以放在句首也可以放在句末，which只能放在句末。
    • 孝敬父母是中华民族的传统美德，这个道理是显而易见的。
      • Respecting senior citizens keeps a chinese conventional virtue, which/as has been known for years.
      • As has been known for years, respecting senior citiyens keeps a chinese conventional virtue.

写作中的使用

修饰限定名词,达到扩充句子内容的目的。

  • Raising pets cute as well as loyal can reduce loneliness of senior citizens who have no offspring to take care of them.
  • Reading books such as Little Prince, Gone with the Wind and I have a Dream can decrease pressure of all children and adults.
  • Wuzhen lying near the river from Beijing to Hangzhou is a water town of Zhejiang Province.
  • I, who is proparing for the examination of postgraduate, miss you being busy at home very much.
  • When I went to the library yesterday, a girl whose boyfriend looked like Mr. Wang sat in front of me.
  • 外表看着邋遢的他内心是高尚的。 He whose appearance looks indecent keeps noble in his mind.
  • 曾经全力以赴去实现梦想的人，即使没有取得成功也不会感到遗憾，这很重要。Those who have spared no efforts to realize their dreams fail to feel pitiful. Although they never succeed, which is of great significance.
  • 课堂上不集中精力希望通过考试的学生常常发现结果会差强人意。Students failing to concentrate in class but wishing to pass the examination find that the consequence is less impressive.
